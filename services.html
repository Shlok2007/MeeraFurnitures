Our Services

Meera Furnitures isn’t just about selling furniture – we believe in providing a complete experience from concept to installation. Our team works with you at every stage to ensure your furniture fits perfectly into your life and your space.

Custom Furniture Design

Have a specific piece in mind? Our designers translate your ideas into custom furniture that reflects your personality. From size and finish to style and functionality, every aspect is tailored just for you.

Delivery

We provide safe and reliable delivery across Mumbai, Thane and the surrounding areas. Your furniture is handled with care from our workshop to your doorstep, giving you peace of mind.

Installation

Our skilled technicians will assemble and install your furniture on‑site. Whether it’s a modular wardrobe or a complex bed frame, we ensure everything is fitted correctly and securely.

About Us

Our Story

Founded in 2018, Meera Furnitures began as a modest workshop in Kalwa, Thane. What started as a passion for carpentry soon grew into a full‑fledged manufacturing unit that now serves families and businesses across the region. Over the past seven years, we’ve built a reputation for delivering quality furniture that stands the test of time.

As a family‑owned business, we believe in treating every customer like a part of our own family. Our growth has been driven by word‑of‑mouth and the trust our customers place in us. We continue to expand our catalogue while retaining the craftsmanship and personal touch that define us.

Our Values

Quality Craftsmanship

Every piece is meticulously built using premium materials and traditional techniques.

Sustainable Sourcing

We source our wood responsibly and strive to minimise waste in our production process.

Customer First

Your satisfaction is our priority. We listen to your needs and customise solutions accordingly.

Craftsmanship

Our artisans blend timeless joinery with modern design. Whether it’s a sleek computer table or a classic wardrobe, the dedication to detail remains the same. We take pride in each dovetail joint, polished surface and carefully selected grain pattern that together create furniture worthy of your home.

Beyond the looks, we build for durability. Solid wood frames, secure fittings and quality finishes ensure that your purchase brings joy and comfort for years to come.
